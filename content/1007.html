How to Disable Bing Search Result on StartMenu in Windows 10

1. Search and Run Registry Editor as Administrator

2.Now Paste this Following Path to Registry Editor Address Bar...

Copy Path

3. Right click on "Search" folder and Create new DWORD 32-bit key called "BingSearchEnabled".

4. Double click on "BingSearchEnabled" and Make sure that it's value data is "0".

5. Restart your Computer.

6. And It's Done.

--- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

 

Back to Default

1. Follow 1 and 2 step.
2. Delete "BingSearchEnabled" key.

3. Restart your Computer

4. And It's Done.
