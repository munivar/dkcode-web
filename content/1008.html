How to Delete/Disable Cortana in Windows 10

Let’s get straight to the point. How do you disable Cortana? Ok Previously, Windows allowed the option to turn off Cortana. But, now there’s none. Although Cortana has been separated from the Start menu, now it just lies vaguely on the taskbar. Moreover, Cortana has become restrictive now and it only uses Bing and Edge over everything. The only way to remove it now is via the registry editor.

1. Search and Run Registry editor as Administrator.

2.Now Paste this Following Path to Registry Editor Address Bar...

Copy Path

3. Right click on "Windows" and Create New key called "Windows Search".
Skip this step if "Windows Search" folder is already available.

4. Now, Right click on "Windows Search" and Create new DWORD 32-bit key Called "AllowCortana".

5. Double click on "AllowCortana" and Make sure that it's value data is "0".

6. Restart your Computer.

7. It's Done.

--- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

 

Back to Default

1. Follow 1 and 2 step and Delete "AllowCortana" key.

2. Restart yout Computer.
3. And It's Done.

Note:- This Trick will also Delete Cortana from your Taskbar.
