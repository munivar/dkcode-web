How to Show Seconds in Windows Taskbar Clock (10:30:09 AM)

1. Search Registry Editor And Run the Registry Editor

2.Now Paste this Following Path to Registry Editor Address Bar...

Copy Path

3. Right Click on "Advanced" folder and Create a New DWORD 32-bit Key Called "ShowSecondsInSystemClock".

4. Double Click on "ShowSecondsInSystemClock" And Set Value Data "1" And Click on "OK".

5. Close Registry Editor and Restart your Computer.

6. It's Done

--- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

 

How to Remove Seconds from Taskbar Clock (10:30:10 AM)

1. Follow 1 and 2 Step and Delete "ShowSecondsInSystemClock" Key.

2. Confirm your Key Deletion and Click "Yes".

3. Restart your Computer.
4. And It's Done.
