Direct Login Password Screen (Skip Blurry Lock Screen)

I was Never a Fan of The Blurry Lock Screen. It Forces an Additional Swipe-up or Button Click before you can Enter a Password. I will Show you How to Disable it.

1. Search Registry Editor And Run the Registry Editor

2.Now Paste this Following Path to Registry Editor Address Bar...

Copy Path

3. Now Right Click on "Windows" folder and Create a New Key Called "Personalization". Skip This step If "Personalization" Folder is Already Available.

4. Right Click on "Personalization" folder and Create a New DWORD 32-bit Key Called "NoLockScreen".

5. Double Click on "NoLockScreen" key and Set Value Data "1" and Click on "OK".

6. Restart your Computer.
7. It's Done.

--- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

 

Back to Default

1. Follow 1 and 2 Step and In Personalization Folder, Delete the "NoLockScreen" Key.

2. Restart your Computer.
3. And It's Done.
