How to Hide Drives from File Explorer in Windows 10

1. Search and Run Registry Editor as Administrator.

2.Now Paste this Following Path to Registry Editor Address Bar...

Copy Path

3. Right click on "Explorer" folder and Create new DWORD 32-bit key Called "NoDrives".

4. Double click on "NoDrives" and Tick "Decimal" and set value data of your drive (value in Decimal).

--- --- Drive Value in Decimal --- ---

B : 2
C : 4

Y : 16777216

ALL : 67108863
--- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

5. In my case I want to Hide "Drive : X". so, i was set value data "8388608" and click on "OK".
Don't forget to tick "Decimal" in Base.

6. Restart your Computer.
7. And it's Done.

Hide Multiple Drives

1. If you want hide Multiple Drives. so, you can First get total of your two Drives. You can done this with 2 and more Drives.

2. In my case I want to hide My C and X Drives.

3. so, The total of C and X drive is "8388612". Now, Enter this Total in value data.
Make sure to tick "Desimal" base.

4. Restart your Computer.
5. And It's Done.

--- --- --- --- --- --- --- ---

Unhide Drives

1. Simply, Delete the "NoDrives" Key.

2. Restart your Computer and Your Drives Unhide Sucessfully.
3. And it's Done.
