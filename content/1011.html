How to Remove Any Grub Bootloader from Windows Startup

Sometime, People try new OS(Oprating System) like Linux,Android Emulator. this all OS comes with Grub/Grub2 Bootloader, Bootloader help to swith one OS to Another in Dual boot. Now, Some People need back to Default setting. so, they delete another OS but the problem is Grub is not delete Automatically. Grub is Menually Deletion Process. so, I am going to show you how to delete grub bootloader from startup...

1. Open CMD (Command Prompt) as Administrator.

2. Go to "diskpart".

3. Now type "list volume".

4. select "System" volume by typing Volume number. in my case system volume number is "5". so, type "select volume 5" and hit "Enter".

5. now assign any letter you want, in my case i want to assign letter "M" by Typing "assign letter=m".

6. Go to Task Manager.

7. Create New Task by clicking top left side "File" button.

8. Now click on "Browse".

9. Navigate EFI folder in your "M" letter assigned Drive and Delete the OS name Folder.

10. Now get Back to CMD and Remove letter "M" by Typing "remove letter=m". And hit "Enter".

11. Restart your Computer
12. And It's Done.
